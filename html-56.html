Amazing facts About India

India has the largest postal network in the world with over 1,55,015 post offices

The wettest inhabitated place in the world is Mawsynram, a village on Khasi Hills, in Meghalaya.

The Bandra Worli Sealink has steel wires equal to the earth's circumference. It took a total of 2,57,00,000 man hours for it's completion. It weighs as much as 50,000 African elephants.

Chail in Himachal Pradesh had the highest cricket pitch in the world. It is situated at an elevation of 2,444 meters (above the sea level).

Science day in Switzerland is dedicated to Ex-Indian President, A.P.J. Abdul Kalam.
