World's First Automobile

The Benz Patent-Motorwagen N1

Specifications

Weight265 kilograms
Maximum speed16 kmph
EngineWater Cooled


The Benz Patent-Motorwagen N1, the first car in the world, was built by German Engineer and Inventor Karl Benz.

In 1906, Karl Benz donated his car to the German Museum in Munich, Germany.

In 1936, three copies of the car were built for the Mercedes Benz Museum, the Museum of Technology in Vienna and the Transport Museum in Dresden.
